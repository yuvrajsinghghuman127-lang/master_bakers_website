🏡 Our Beginnings

A Bakery Born from Passion

Master Bakers first opened its doors in 1992 on the beloved Hudson Lane, in the heart of Delhi's North Campus. What started as a small neighbourhood bakery with a handful of tables and a single oven has grown into one of the most cherished dining spots for generations of Delhi University students and local residents.

The idea was simple: serve honestly made food in a warm, welcoming space. No pretence, no fuss — just good bread, great coffee, and the kind of ambiance that makes you want to stay a little longer.

Over the decades, we've watched students become alumni, children become parents, and Hudson Lane transform — but through it all, the smell of freshly baked goods and the warmth of our space has remained constant. Our regulars span generations, and that continuity is something we're incredibly proud of.

Today, Master Bakers continues to serve the same honest, flavourful food it always has — with a few modern touches like QR-based ordering to make your experience even smoother.

📅 Our Journey

Milestones Over the Years

1992

The Beginning

Master Bakers opens its first outlet on Hudson Lane, North Campus, with 4 tables and a wood-fired oven.

Late 1990s

Word Spreads

DU students make Master Bakers their study spot of choice. The cold coffee and club sandwich become campus legends.

2000s

Expanding the Menu

We introduce a full hot beverages menu and expand our snacks range, including wraps, rolls, and grilled sandwiches.

2010s

Renovation & Growth

The bakery gets a warm interior makeover, expanding seating capacity and introducing European-style pastries and cakes.

2024

Going Digital

We launch our QR-based table ordering system, bringing the full menu to your smartphone for a seamless dine-in experience.
